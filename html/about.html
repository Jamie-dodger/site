About Us

We are open to all levels of experience and hope to offer something for everyone. Our training consists of drills and games so our players have the most experience possible in order to have the most confidence possible heading into games and tournaments.



Our volleyball club is based in harrotown and competes on a national scale, frequently playing away games all over the UK.











We have regular mixed friendlies and other social events within the club and with other local clubs!













Our club is proud to include mens, womens and juniors teams who all compete in the Yorkshire area!
